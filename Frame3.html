What we will do with proceeds

A portion of the proceeds for this book go to United We Dream, a non-profit focused on the rights of immigrants in the US. Their site is full of invaluable resources and we encourage you to learn more about their mission at United We Dream

What are we aiming for

Pride in multicultural identity
A bilingual (Spanish-English) illustrated children's book that speaks to the importance of multicultural identities and birthright citizenship in the U.S.
